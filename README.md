# ViciousBets
  ViciousBets is a head-to-head sports betting platform where players compete against one another by placing picks on real player statlines, with the player with the most accurate pick winning the pot. 

  The target users for ViciousBets are sports fans who enjoy the strategy inherent to betting but want a competitive twist, a feature that most platforms fail to offer. The player vs. player nature of ViciousBets additionally provides a fairer betting environment, giving skeptical players the transparency many other sites lack.
